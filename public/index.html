My Story

As a junior full-stack developer, I bring a solid understanding of both front-end and back-end development technologies. On the front-end, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with experience in popular frameworks like React, Next and React Native for building user interfaces. I have worked extensively with Material-UI (Mui), Chakra, Ant Design, and Tailwind CSS to create responsive and visually appealing designs, leveraging their rich component libraries and theming capabilities.
In terms of back-end development, I have experience with Node.js and Express, and I am particularly adept at using the NestJS framework to build robust, scalable, and maintainable server-side applications. I have integrated NestJS with databases like MongoDB and PostgreSQL, implementing RESTful APIs and incorporating authentication and authorization mechanisms using tools like JWT and OAuth.
I am comfortable working with version control systems like Git and have experience deploying applications using platforms like AWS and Heroku. My development approach emphasizes clean code, scalability, and performance optimization.
While I continue to expand my knowledge and skills, I am eager to contribute to projects, collaborate with team members, and learn from experienced developers to further enhance my capabilities as a full-stack developer.
